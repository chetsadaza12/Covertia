# Members Settings

The **Members Settings** panel is designed for multi-user collaboration and team management within Covertia. It allows account administrators to invite team members, share browser profiles, and assign custom access permissions.

---

## Subscription Requirement

To ensure system security and cover cloud synchronization resource costs, team collaboration and member management features are restricted by subscription tiers.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Plan Availability**
> This feature is only available on paid premium plans. It cannot be accessed or configured when using a free account or when logged in as a standard member account.

If you require multi-user access or profile sharing features for your team, please upgrade your subscription plan via your account dashboard.
